Help & Support

How do I cancel a booking?

To cancel a booking, go to the Orders tab, select the booking you want to cancel, and tap on the 'Cancel' button. Please note that cancellations made less than 2 hours before the scheduled service may incur a cancellation fee.

How are QuickFix service providers verified?

All QuickFix service providers undergo a rigorous verification process. This includes verification of their CNIC, professional certifications, experience, background checks, and skill assessment. We also conduct regular quality checks and monitor customer feedback to ensure high service standards.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with the service, you can raise a complaint through the 'Raise Issue' tab in the Support section within 24 hours of service completion. Our team will investigate the matter and offer an appropriate solution, which may include service re-do, partial refund, or full refund depending on the situation.

How do I pay for services?

QuickFix offers multiple payment options: cash payment after service completion, wallet payment using your QuickFix wallet balance, or online payment via credit/debit cards or bank transfers. For certain premium services, a partial advance payment may be required at the time of booking.

Do services come with any warranty?

Yes, most services come with a warranty period ranging from 7 to 30 days depending on the type of service. During this period, if you face any issues related to the service that was performed, we will fix it free of charge. The warranty details for each service are mentioned in the service description.
